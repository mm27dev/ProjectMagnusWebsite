Mathematics
is the foundational science of structure, order, relation, and change, involving the study of numbers, shapes, and patterns. It uses logic, abstraction, and precise reasoning to understand quantitative relationships, ranging from daily calculations to complex and abstract systems.

Chess
is a two-player, turn-based strategy board game played on a 64-square, grid, where the goal is to checkmate the opponent’s king. Each player controls 16 pieces—king, queen, two rooks, two bishops, two knights, and eight pawns—which move in distinct ways, with White moving first

We combined both of them to make Project Magnus.
